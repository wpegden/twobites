- `reference.tex:331-334` (`lem:fiber_and_degree`): the proof is only sketched. For Lean planning, this should be split into routine concentration components: fiber size concentration, degree concentration in `G_R`/`G_B`, concentration of `|N_v|`, codegree bounds `|N_v ‚ą© N_w| = O(log^3 n)`, and projected codegree bounds.
