\begin{helper}
Let \(I\) be a finite set of size \(k\), let \(W\subseteq I\), and let
\(\pi\) be a map from the ambient vertex set to a finite projection set.  Then
\[
|W|-|\pi(W)|\le k-|\pi(I)|.
\]
\end{helper}
\begin{proof}
Every point of \(\pi(I)\) is either the projection of a point of \(W\), or the
projection of a point of \(I\setminus W\).  Hence
\[
|\pi(I)|\le |\pi(W)|+|\pi(I\setminus W)|
  \le |\pi(W)|+|I\setminus W|.
\]
Since \(W\subseteq I\), we have \(|I\setminus W|=|I|-|W|=k-|W|\).  Substituting
this into the preceding inequality and rearranging gives
\[
|W|-|\pi(W)|\le k-|\pi(I)|.
\]
\end{proof}
