For a fixed blue-side support-label summation, suppose the branch/transcript
cells are pairwise disjoint inside each fixed pair of support labels and each
cell has the cylinder charge supplied by
\(\noderef{FixedSetHistoryCellFixedCylinderCharge}\).  Then summing the cell
charges over the finite branch/transcript family bounds the conditional
probability of the corresponding fixed-count event.
\end{helper}
\begin{proof}
SKETCH:
Use the finite branch/transcript summation lemma
\(\noderef{FixedSetHistoryCellBranchTranscriptSummation}\).  The disjointness
of the cells prevents multiplicity, and the fixed-cylinder charge gives the
termwise probability contribution for each cell.  Summing those contributions
over the realized finite family gives the claimed fixed-count cell
summation.
\end{proof}
