\begin{helper}[Fixed-vertex lifted-size tail transfer]
Fix \(n,m,q\), a marked set
\(K\subseteq \operatorname{Fin}(m)\times\operatorname{Fin}(m)\) with
\(|K|=q\), and parameters \(p\ge0\) and \(0<\delta\le1/2\).  Assume
\(n\le m^2\), and put
\[
  \mu=nq/m^2 .
\]
If \(\mu\) is within relative error \(\delta\) of \(pn\), then the number of
injections \(\phi:\operatorname{Fin}(n)\hookrightarrow
\operatorname{Fin}(m)\times\operatorname{Fin}(m)\) for which
\[
  \left|\,|\operatorname{im}(\phi)\cap K|-pn\,\right|>3\delta\,pn
\]
is at most
\[
 \left[
  \exp\!\left(-(\delta+(1-\delta)\log(1-\delta))(1-\delta)pn\right)
  +
  \exp\!\left(-(((1+\delta)\log(1+\delta)-\delta)(1-\delta)pn)\right)
 \right]
 \left|\operatorname{Inj}\!\left(
   \operatorname{Fin}(n),
   \operatorname{Fin}(m)\times\operatorname{Fin}(m)
 \right)\right|.
\]
\end{helper}
\begin{proof}
By \(\noderef{FiberAndDegreeLiftedSizeImageHypergeometricTail}\), the
injection count outside the interval
\([(1-\delta)\mu,(1+\delta)\mu]\) is bounded by the same two Chernoff factors
with \(\mu\) in place of \((1-\delta)pn\), times the total number of
injections.  The assumption
\(\noderef{WithinRelativeError}(\delta,pn,\mu)\) gives
\[
  (1-\delta)pn\le \mu\le (1+\delta)pn .
\]
Therefore any image-hit count lying inside
\([(1-\delta)\mu,(1+\delta)\mu]\) lies inside
\([(1-3\delta)pn,(1+3\delta)pn]\), by the elementary inequalities
\((1-\delta)^2\ge 1-3\delta\) and \((1+\delta)^2\le1+3\delta\) for
\(0\le\delta\le1/2\).  Hence a failure of the \(3\delta\)-relative-error
bound around \(pn\) is contained in the hypergeometric upper-or-lower tail
around \(\mu\).  Finally,
\(\noderef{ChernoffFiberTailExponentsPositive}\) shows both Chernoff
exponents are positive, so replacing \(\mu\) by the smaller lower bound
\((1-\delta)pn\) only increases the displayed exponential upper bound.
\end{proof}
