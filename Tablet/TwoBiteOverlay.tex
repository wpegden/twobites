\begin{definition}
For a two-bite sample \(\omega\), the two-bite overlay
\(\widetilde G(\omega)\) is the colored red-blue multigraph on \(V(G)\) with
separate edge sets
\[
\widetilde E_R(G)=
\{uv:\pi_R(u)\pi_R(v)\in E(G_R)\}
\]
and
\[
\widetilde E_B(G)=
\{uv:\pi_B(u)\pi_B(v)\in E(G_B)\}.
\]
Edges in \(\widetilde E_R(G)\) are red and edges in \(\widetilde E_B(G)\) are
blue.  If \(uv\in \widetilde E_R(G)\cap\widetilde E_B(G)\), both colored
copies are retained; the overlay is not collapsed to a simple graph before the
deletion step.
\end{definition}
