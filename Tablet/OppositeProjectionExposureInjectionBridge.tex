\begin{helper}
Fix \(U_0,V_0\subseteq\operatorname{Fin}n\), a red graph \(G_R\), a row map
\(\rho:\operatorname{Fin}n\to\operatorname{Fin}m\), and an exposure
\(\eta:U_0\to\operatorname{Fin}m\).  Put \(S=\eta(U_0)\).  Let
\(X_{\mathrm{all},\eta}\) be the set of injections
\(\phi:\operatorname{Fin}n\hookrightarrow
\operatorname{Fin}m\times\operatorname{Fin}m\) satisfying
\[
(\phi(v))_1=\rho(v)\quad\text{for every }v
\]
and
\[
(\phi(u))_2=\eta(u)\quad\text{for every }u\in U_0.
\]
For each finite set \(T\subseteq\operatorname{Fin}n\), let
\(X_{\mathrm{good},\eta}(T)\subseteq X_{\mathrm{all},\eta}\) be the subset
where \((\phi(v))_2\in S\) for every \(v\in T\).  Then the conditional
probability, under the conditioning \(G_R,\rho,\eta\), that at least \(t\)
vertices of \(V_0\) have blue coordinate in \(S\) is at most
\[
\sum_{\substack{T\subseteq V_0\\ |T|=t}}
\frac{|X_{\mathrm{good},\eta}(T)|}{|X_{\mathrm{all},\eta}|}.
\]
\end{helper}
\begin{proof}
Unfold \(\noderef{TwoBiteConditionalProbability}\).  If the conditioning
event has zero \(\noderef{TwoBiteEventProbability}\), the conditional
probability is defined to be \(0\), and the displayed upper bound is
immediate.

Assume the conditioning event has nonzero probability.  Expanding
\(\noderef{TwoBiteEventProbability}\) over the finite two-bite sample space,
the red graph \(G_R\), the red row map \(\rho\), and the exposed blue
coordinates \(\eta\) are fixed by the conditioning event.  The remaining
blue graph contributes a common total factor, and the uniform injection
weight is common to all admissible injections.  After canceling these common
factors in the conditional probability, any event depending only on the
injection is measured by the corresponding cardinality ratio inside
\(X_{\mathrm{all},\eta}\).

Let
\[
W_\eta(\phi)=|\{v\in V_0:(\phi(v))_2\in S\}|.
\]
If \(W_\eta(\phi)\ge t\), then the set
\(\{v\in V_0:(\phi(v))_2\in S\}\) contains a \(t\)-element subset
\(T\subseteq V_0\).  Therefore the tail event is contained in the finite
union, over all \(T\in\operatorname{powersetCard}(t,V_0)\), of the events
defining \(X_{\mathrm{good},\eta}(T)\).  Applying the finite union bound to
the cardinality ratios gives
\[
\Pr(W_\eta\ge t\mid G_R,\rho,\eta)
\le
\sum_{T\in\operatorname{powersetCard}(t,V_0)}
\frac{|X_{\mathrm{good},\eta}(T)|}{|X_{\mathrm{all},\eta}|}.
\]
This is the claimed bridge from the conditional tail probability to the
fixed-\(T\) injection-count ratios.
\end{proof}
