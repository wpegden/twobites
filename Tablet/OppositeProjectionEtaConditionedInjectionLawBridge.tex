\begin{helper}
Fix \(U_0\subseteq\operatorname{Fin} n\), a red graph \(G_R\), a row map
\(\rho:\operatorname{Fin} n\to\operatorname{Fin} m\), an exposed blue map
\(\eta:U_0\to\operatorname{Fin} m\), and a predicate \(P\) on injections
\(\phi:\operatorname{Fin} n\hookrightarrow
\operatorname{Fin}m\times\operatorname{Fin}m\).  Let \(X_\eta\) be the set of
injections satisfying \((\phi(v))_1=\rho(v)\) for every \(v\) and
\((\phi(u))_2=\eta(u)\) for every \(u\in U_0\), and let
\(X_P=\{\phi\in X_\eta:P(\phi)\}\).  The two-bite event probability of samples
with red graph \(G_R\), first-coordinate map \(\rho\), exposed coordinates
\(\eta\), and injection satisfying \(P\), equals
\[
  \operatorname{GnpGraphWeight}_p(G_R)\,
  \operatorname{UniformInjectionWeight}_{n,m}\, |X_P|.
\]
\end{helper}
\begin{proof}
Unfold \(\noderef{TwoBiteEventProbability}\) and the sample weight
\(\noderef{TwoBiteSampleWeight}\).  The finite sum is over triples
\((G'_R,G_B,\phi)\).  The event forces \(G'_R=G_R\), forces the
first-coordinate and exposed-coordinate conditions on \(\phi\), and then
retains exactly the injections in \(X_P\).  For each retained injection the
factor \(\operatorname{UniformInjectionWeight}_{n,m}\) is constant, and the
red graph contributes the fixed factor \(\operatorname{GnpGraphWeight}_p(G_R)\).
The remaining sum over the blue graph is \(1\) by
\(\noderef{GnpGraphWeightSumOne}\).  Hence the total mass is the common
factor times the number of retained injections, namely
\(\operatorname{GnpGraphWeight}_p(G_R)
\operatorname{UniformInjectionWeight}_{n,m}|X_P|\).
\end{proof}
