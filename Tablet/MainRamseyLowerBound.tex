\begin{theorem}
For every real \(\eta>0\), there is an integer \(k_0\) such that for every
integer \(k\ge k_0\) there is an integer \(N\) with
\[
\noderef{RamseyWitness}(N,k)
\]
and
\[
\left(\frac12-\eta\right)\noderef{RamseyScale}(k)\le (N:\mathbb R).
\]
Unfolding \(\noderef{RamseyScale}\) and \(\noderef{RamseyWitness}\), this is
the usual \(\eta\)-form of the lower bound
\[
R(3,k)\ge \left(\frac12+o(1)\right)\frac{k^2}{\log k}.
\]
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
We prove the formal statement directly from the two child theorems
\(\noderef{MainIndependence}\) and \(\noderef{MainToRamseyWitness}\).

First record that \(\noderef{MainIndependence}\) supplies exactly the
hypothesis required by \(\noderef{MainToRamseyWitness}\).  Namely, for every
real \(\varepsilon>0\), \(\noderef{MainIndependence}\) gives an integer
\(n_0\) such that for every integer \(n\ge n_0\) there exists a simple graph
\(G\) on \(\mathrm{Fin}(n)\) with
\[
G.\mathrm{CliqueFree}(3)
\]
and
\[
\noderef{IndependenceNumberLess}
\left(G,(1+\varepsilon)\sqrt{(n:\mathbb R)\log(n:\mathbb R)}\right).
\]
This is word-for-word the input hypothesis \(h_{\mathrm{main}}\) in
\(\noderef{MainToRamseyWitness}\).

Now fix a real number \(\eta>0\).  Apply
\(\noderef{MainToRamseyWitness}\) with
\[
h_{\mathrm{main}}=\noderef{MainIndependence}
\]
and then apply the resulting conclusion to this \(\eta\).  We obtain an
integer \(k_0\) such that, for every integer \(k\ge k_0\), there exists an
integer \(N\) satisfying the two conclusions
\[
\noderef{RamseyWitness}(N,k)
\]
and
\[
\left(\frac12-\eta\right)\noderef{RamseyScale}(k)\le (N:\mathbb R).
\]
This is exactly the pair of assertions required by the theorem for the fixed
\(\eta\).  Since \(\eta>0\) was arbitrary, the quantified statement follows.

The final explanatory sentence in the statement is not an additional formal
step.  It is the interpretation of the same quantified conclusion using the
definition of \(\noderef{RamseyWitness}\), the scale definition
\(\noderef{RamseyScale}(k)=k^2/\log k\), and the asymptotic scale comparison
recorded in \(\noderef{RamseyScaleConversion}\).
\end{proof}
