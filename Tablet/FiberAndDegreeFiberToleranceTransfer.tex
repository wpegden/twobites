\begin{helper}
Let \(\eta\le\varepsilon\).  If all red fibers and all blue fibers satisfy
the \(\eta\)-relative error estimate around \((\log n)^2\), then the same red
and blue fiber clauses hold with tolerance \(\varepsilon\).
\end{helper}
\begin{proof}
For every fiber, the target \((\log n)^2\) is nonnegative.  The
\(\eta\)-relative error estimate says
\[
|X-(\log n)^2|\le \eta(\log n)^2.
\]
Since \(\eta\le\varepsilon\), multiplying by the nonnegative target gives
\(\eta(\log n)^2\le\varepsilon(\log n)^2\).  Thus
\[
|X-(\log n)^2|\le\varepsilon(\log n)^2.
\]
This argument applies independently to every red fiber and every blue fiber.
\end{proof}
