\begin{helper}
Let \(U_0,V_0,T\subseteq\operatorname{Fin}n\), let
\(\rho:\operatorname{Fin}n\to\operatorname{Fin}m\), and let
\(\eta:U_0\to\operatorname{Fin}m\).  Put \(S=\eta(U_0)\) and
\[
R_i=\{v:\rho(v)=i\}.
\]
Let \(X_{\mathrm{all},\eta}\) and \(X_{\mathrm{good},\eta}(T)\) be the
eta-conditioned global denominator and numerator injection sets from
\(\noderef{OppositeProjectionExposureRowProductBound}\).  For each row
\(i\), let \(d_i\) be the number of injective maps \(R_i\to\operatorname{Fin}m\)
that agree with \(\eta\) on \(U_0\cap R_i\).  Let \(g_i\) be the number of
such maps that also send every vertex of \(T\cap R_i\) into \(S\).  Assume
\(T\subseteq V_0\), assume the \(U_0\)-rows are disjoint from the \(V_0\)-rows,
and assume \(X_{\mathrm{all},\eta}\) is nonempty.  Then
\[
|X_{\mathrm{all},\eta}|=\prod_i d_i,\qquad
|X_{\mathrm{good},\eta}(T)|=\prod_i g_i,
\]
every \(d_i\) is nonzero, every row quotient \(g_i/d_i\) is nonnegative,
\[
\frac{g_i}{d_i}\le \left(\frac{|S|}{m}\right)^{|T\cap R_i|}
\quad\text{for every }i,
\]
and
\[
\frac{|X_{\mathrm{good},\eta}(T)|}{|X_{\mathrm{all},\eta}|}
=\prod_i \frac{g_i}{d_i}.
\]
\end{helper}
\begin{proof}
The global injection \(\phi\) has first coordinate fixed by \(\rho\).  Thus
its remaining data are exactly the independent choices of blue coordinates in
each row \(R_i\).  Within a row the second coordinates must be injective;
between different rows, injectivity is automatic because the first
coordinates are different.  This gives the denominator factorization
\(|X_{\mathrm{all},\eta}|=\prod_i d_i\).  Adding the condition that
\((\phi(v))_2\in S\) for \(v\in T\) only changes the row \(i\) count by
replacing \(d_i\) with \(g_i\), so
\(|X_{\mathrm{good},\eta}(T)|=\prod_i g_i\).

Since \(X_{\mathrm{all},\eta}\) is nonempty, the product \(\prod_i d_i\) is
nonzero, and therefore every row denominator \(d_i\) is nonzero.  The row
quotients \(g_i/d_i\) are nonnegative because they are quotients of
nonnegative finite counts with positive denominators.  Dividing the two
product formulae and using the nonzero row denominators gives
\[
\frac{|X_{\mathrm{good},\eta}(T)|}{|X_{\mathrm{all},\eta}|}
=\prod_i \frac{g_i}{d_i}.
\]

If \(T\cap R_i=\varnothing\), then \(g_i=d_i\), so \(g_i/d_i=1\).  If
\(T\cap R_i\ne\varnothing\), row-disjointness implies
\(U_0\cap R_i=\varnothing\), hence the eta condition imposes no constraint in
that row.  The ordinary row estimate from
\(\noderef{OppositeProjectionRowInjectionCounting}\) gives
\[
\frac{g_i}{d_i}\le \left(\frac{|S|}{m}\right)^{|T\cap R_i|}.
\]
Together these observations give all asserted factorization, nonzero,
nonnegativity, and row-bound conclusions.
\end{proof}
