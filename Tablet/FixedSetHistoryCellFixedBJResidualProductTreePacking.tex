\begin{helper}
Fix one blue support label \(B\) and one red label \(J\).  Let \(U\) be the
terminal coordinate set, let \(o\) be a nodup terminal order with
\(\operatorname{toFinset}(o)=U\), let \(\mathcal T\) be the finite
branch-label type, and let \(\mathcal P\) be the finite transcript set.  For
a realized pair \(c=(\beta,\tau)\), write
\[
R_c=R_\beta,\qquad P_c=P_\tau,\qquad A_c=A_\tau,\qquad Z_c=Z_\tau .
\]
Assume \(B\subseteq U\) and \(|B|=u_B\).  Assume the fixed-\((B,J)\)
geometry: for every realized \(c=(\beta,\tau)\) with
\(\tau\in\mathcal P\),
\[
|R_\beta|=u_R,\qquad R_\beta\subseteq U,\qquad B\cap R_\beta=\emptyset,
\]
\[
P_\tau,A_\tau\subseteq U,\qquad P_\tau\cap A_\tau=\emptyset,\qquad
B\cup R_\beta\subseteq P_\tau,
\]
and \(P_\tau,A_\tau\) encode the complete terminal blue trace of \(\beta\).
Also assume \(Z_\tau\subseteq A_\tau\) and
\[
L:=\max(0,a-u_R-u_B)\le |Z_\tau|.
\]

Assume the lower-level residual leaf certificate for this fixed \(B,J\):
there is a finite residual leaf set \(\Lambda\), a leaf assignment
\[
\iota:\mathcal T\times\mathcal P\to\Lambda,
\]
and a nonnegative normalized leaf mass
\(\nu:\Lambda\to\mathbb R\).  The assignment is injective on realized
branch/transcript cells, and for every realized cell
\[
p^{|P_\tau|-u_R-u_B}(1-p)^{|A_\tau|-|Z_\tau|}
  \le \nu(\iota(\beta,\tau)).
\]

If \(0\le p\le1/2\), then there are nonnegative masses
\(\rho_{\beta,\tau}\), indexed by all branch/transcript pairs for this fixed
\((B,J)\), such that
\[
\sum_{\beta,\tau\in\mathcal P}\rho_{\beta,\tau}\le1
\]
and, for every realized \(c=(\beta,\tau)\),
\[
p^{|P_c|}(1-p)^{|A_c|}
\le
p^{u_R}p^{u_B}(1-p)^L\rho_{\beta,\tau}.
\]
\end{helper}
\begin{proof}
Invoke \(\noderef{FixedSetHistoryCellFixedBJResidualCertificateConstruction}\)
with the same fixed \(B,J\), terminal order \(o\), branch traces,
transcripts, realized predicate, residual leaf assignment, and residual leaf
mass.  That node converts the leaf-level scan certificate into
cell-indexed residual masses \(\sigma_{\beta,\tau}\) satisfying
\[
0\le\sigma_{\beta,\tau}
\]
for realized allowed pairs,
\[
\sum_{\beta}
  \sum_{\tau\in\mathcal P:\,(\beta,\tau)\text{ realized}}
    \sigma_{\beta,\tau}\le1,
\]
and
\[
p^{|P_\tau|-u_R-u_B}(1-p)^{|A_\tau|-|Z_\tau|}
\le \sigma_{\beta,\tau}
\]
for every realized allowed pair.

Define
\[
\rho_{\beta,\tau}=
\begin{cases}
\sigma_{\beta,\tau},&\text{if }\tau\in\mathcal P
  \text{ and }(\beta,\tau)\text{ is realized},\\
0,&\text{otherwise.}
\end{cases}
\]
Nonnegativity is immediate from the certificate on realized cells and from
the zero definition off realized cells.  The displayed certificate sum gives
\[
\sum_{\beta,\tau\in\mathcal P}\rho_{\beta,\tau}\le1.
\]

Fix a realized allowed pair \(c=(\beta,\tau)\).  From
\(B\cup R_\beta\subseteq P_\tau\) we have both \(B\subseteq P_\tau\) and
\(R_\beta\subseteq P_\tau\).  The fixed geometry gives
\[
|B|=u_B,\qquad |R_\beta|=u_R,\qquad B\cap R_\beta=\emptyset,
\]
\[
Z_\tau\subseteq A_\tau,\qquad P_\tau\cap A_\tau=\emptyset,\qquad
L\le |Z_\tau|.
\]
The residual domination supplied by
\(\noderef{FixedSetHistoryCellFixedBJResidualCertificateConstruction}\)
gives
\[
p^{|P_\tau|-u_R-u_B}(1-p)^{|A_\tau|-|Z_\tau|}
\le \rho_{\beta,\tau}.
\]
Applying \(\noderef{FixedSetHistoryCellRedTermwiseCylinderDomination}\) with
\[
R=R_\beta,\qquad P=P_\tau,\qquad A=A_\tau,\qquad Z=Z_\tau
\]
therefore yields
\[
p^{|P_\tau|}(1-p)^{|A_\tau|}
\le
p^{u_R}p^{u_B}(1-p)^L\rho_{\beta,\tau}.
\]
This is the desired bound for \(c\).  Non-realized pairs have no termwise
claim and receive mass \(0\), so the same family \(\rho\) proves the helper.
\end{proof}
