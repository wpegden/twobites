\begin{helper}[SameColorCodegreeLimitBound]
\label{helper:SameColorCodegreeLimitBound}
Let \(m(n) = \noderef{TwoBiteNaturalReducedVertexCount}(n)\) and let \(p = \noderef{TwoBiteEdgeProbability}(1/2, n)\).
Let \(T = \lceil \log n \rceil\). The sequence
\[
2 m(n)^2 \binom{m(n)}{T} (p^2)^T
\]
is a \(\noderef{NegligibleProbability}\) sequence.
\end{helper}

\begin{proof}
Unfold \(\noderef{NegligibleProbability}\); we must prove that the displayed
sequence tends to \(0\).  Put \(L=\log n\), \(M=m(n)\), and
\(T=\lceil L\rceil\).  From the hypothesis on \(m\) and the definitions
\(\noderef{TwoBiteNaturalReducedVertexCount}\),
\(\noderef{TwoBiteReducedVertexCount}\), and \(\noderef{TwoBiteStretch}\), we
have
\[
M=\left\lfloor \frac{n}{L^2}\right\rfloor .
\]
After discarding finitely many \(n\), \(L\ge1\), so the floor bound gives
\[
M\le \frac{n}{L^2}\le n.
\tag{1}
\]
The definition \(\noderef{TwoBiteEdgeProbability}\) with \(\beta=1/2\) gives
\[
p^2=\frac14\,\frac{L}{n}
\tag{2}
\]
for all sufficiently large \(n\), when \(L/n\ge0\).  Also, by the definition
of the ceiling, \(T=\lceil L\rceil\) satisfies
\[
T\ge L.
\tag{3}
\]

By \(\noderef{OppositeProjectionChooseExponentialBound}\),
\[
\binom MT\le\left(\frac{eM}{T}\right)^T.
\]
Multiplying by \((p^2)^T\) and using (1), (2), and (3), the base of the
exponential is bounded by
\[
\frac{eMp^2}{T}
\le
\frac{e}{4L^2}.
\tag{4}
\]
For large \(n\), the right side of (4) is at most \(1\).  Since \(T\ge L\),
\[
\binom MT(p^2)^T
\le
\left(\frac{e}{4L^2}\right)^T
\le
\left(\frac{e}{4L^2}\right)^L
=
\exp\!\left(L(1-\log 4-2\log L)\right)
\le
\exp\!\left(-\frac12 L\log L\right)
\tag{5}
\]
eventually.

The prefactor is controlled by (1):
\[
2M^2\le2n^2=2\exp(2L).
\tag{6}
\]
Combining (5) and (6), the displayed sequence is eventually at most
\[
2\exp\!\left(2L-\frac12 L\log L\right).
\]
The exponent tends to \(-\infty\), because \(L\log L\) dominates \(L\).
Hence this upper bound tends to \(0\), and the original nonnegative sequence
also tends to \(0\).  This proves the required
\(\noderef{NegligibleProbability}\) statement.
\end{proof}
