# Soundness

A node *N* is **sound** iff the natural-language proof in *N*'s `.tex` rigorously establishes *N*'s `.tex` statement by a detailed, line-by-line checkable proof from the children of *N*.  Soundness requires proofs that are clear and highly detailed, providing a strong roadmap for formalization in Lean; as such, the detail level in the paper is a floor on the detail one expects in a proof that passes soundness.
